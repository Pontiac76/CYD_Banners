One hardware design can replace many
ROM types with the same pin count.
ROM type, size, pin behavior, and chip
select behavior are configurable.

Tested systems include Commodore 64,
VIC-20, PET, 1541 drives, Atari 800XL,
TI-99, CoCo2, BBC Micro and Master,
PC XT, Amiga, and arcade systems.
